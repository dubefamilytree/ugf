    Our Story

    F.I.F University Graduates Fellowship (U.G.F) formerly known as Postgraduates’ Fellowship, a ministry which was started through the inspiration of the Holy Spirit to the Archbishop and the Founder of ZAOGA F.I.F Prof. E. H. Guti in 1991 with the first university on board being Zaoga society at University of Zimbabwe, now called Zaoga on Campus across all. The main mission is for the graduates to preach their peers in order to fulfill our Lord Jesus Christ’s greatest commission, Mathew 28: 16-18. The ministry which has its dominance in Zimbabwe is projected to reach the whole world and where ever it is just like the mother church, it is selfsupporting, self-administering and self-propagating.

    The God of our Father E.H Guti has made it that we Zimbabweans are being sent out through skills transfer and we need to tape on this enormous breakthrough for the fastest growing international ministry. Therefore, in-order to achieve this we plan to create a sub-web page on FIFMI.org with (UGF) Graduates contact database and platform to assist the missionary office’ outreach program, for oversees contacts, tent makers and missionaries in other countries at the click of a button.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Here are some of the questions asked!!

    What is UGF?

    U.G.F is a ministry started by our father Prof E.H Guti with a mission to preach to our peers in differnt universities in order to fulfill our Lord Jesus Christ’s greatest commission. And it branches into differnt universities with the name ZAOGA ON CAMPUS.

    What is the main purpose for UGF?

    • Reaching out to the graduates with the gospel of Jesus Christ.
    • To help the mother church in reaching lost souls, world over through members’ direct preaching and or indirect preaching by way of contributing Mission Fund and other activities needed to achieve the said purpose.
    • To help ZOC activities through advice, spiritual guidance and any other contributions in order to meet their purpose.
    • To come up with creative & networking platforms for the benefit of all members such as Prayer Retreats and GCCs.

    Who started the UGF Ministry?

    F.I.F University Graduates Fellowship (U.G.F) formerly known as Postgraduates’ Fellowship, a ministry which was started through the inspiration of the Holy Spirit to the Archbishop and the Founder of ZAOGA F.I.F Prof. E. H. Guti in 1991.
