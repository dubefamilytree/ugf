Dedication

To the Lord Jesus Christ; And to the Servant and Apostle of God Prof. E. H Guti; we believe without any iota of doubt that God indeed raised a prophet, an apostle, a father of all and above all, a bond servant of Christ who does not do things from his own mind but from God; To our mother Dr E Guti for encouraging us and this ministry to take up the vision and have the spirit of this church; Finally to all postgraduates and undergraduates who want to rise up and be counted throughout Zimbabwe and the nations across as sons & daughters of the great apostle E. H Guti.

Foreword

It is of paramount importance for any member or leader of this ministry to know that this ministry has come to this far through the obedience of our father Apostle Prof. E.H. Guti to the simple message (FEAR NOT SIN NOT) that he received from God more than half a dozen of decades ago and also due to prayer, fasting and love.

In this ministry, the founder’s main message, “Seek ye first the Kingdom of God and His righteousness and all these things will be added unto you”, Mathew 6:33, makes this ministry unique. The message has been preached from time immemorial without dilution regardless of the fact that the preaching methodology is changing due to such things as instruments, internet and gospel channels like Ezekiel T.V among other things. Any member or leader who shall come into this ministry shall not therefore seek to change the message due to technological and methodological dynamics instead they should pass the baton stick as it is to the next generation.

Leaders in this ministry are not supposed to be seen fighting with the main constitution (rules and policy) of F.I.F. as it has to be clear to any one that this ministry is a byproduct of F.I.F as such it has to be seen to be augmenting the rules and policy of our church. Any leader or member therefore who shall knowingly move a motion to propose issues that are contrary to rules and policy should know that he or she has denied the faith due to the simple fact that our God is not the author of confusion. The other leaders or members should correct that lost member with love knowing that they can also make similar mistakes.

God sets people into leadership positions and disempowers them, thus, once one is made to be a leader in this ministry, one should strive to serve faithfully in this world where the world is crying for faithful and true leaders (Guti, 1995). A great leader according to the teachings of our Lord Jesus Christ is the one who reduce one’s self to be a servant. Prof. Guti always says that a good leader is the one who is not a boss.

Leaders and members of this ministry are implored to pray and plan for this ministry. Failure to plan and pray is a good enough recipe for failure. Prayer ensures that God is involved in our plans and hence success is guaranteed. Without God, planners should know that their plans are but vanity. A good example can be drawn from our father Prof. E.H Guti who declined to accept money and buses from a white man prior to consulting his God in 1972 in America.

INTRODUCTION

1.1 History and Formation

F.I.F University Graduates Fellowship (U.G.F) formerly known as Postgraduates’ Fellowship, a ministry which was started through the inspiration of the Holy Spirit to the Archbishop and the Founder of ZAOGA F.I.F Prof. E. H. Guti in 1991 with the first university on board being Zaoga society at University of Zimbabwe, now called Zaoga on Campus across all.

The main mission is for the graduates to preach their peers in order to fulfill our Lord Jesus Christ’s greatest commission, Mathew 28: 16-18. The ministry which has its dominance in Zimbabwe is projected to reach the whole world and where ever it is just like the mother church, it is self￾supporting, self-administering and self-propagating.

The God of our Father E.H Guti has made it that we Zimbabweans are being sent out through skills transfer and we need to tape on this enormous breakthrough for the fastest growing international ministry. Therefore, in-order to achieve this we plan to create a sub-web page on FIFMI.org with (UGF) Graduates contact database and platform to assist the missionary office’ outreach program, for oversees contacts, tent makers and missionaries in other countries at the click of a button.

While, in July 2012, after successfully putting our heads together we elected a steering committee and proposed a name change which was communicated to our Father Prof. E.H Guti. A discussion was carried out during the meeting of where to start from after the Postgraduates Fellowship died a natural death due to a leadership vacuum and lack of membership, which gave birth to the idea of incorporating all Zaoga on Campus (ZOC) undergraduates’ university students.

As agreed by all members of the University Graduate Fellowship and the following requirements were spelt out; The FIFMI University Graduate Fellowship shall or would comprise of:

  • Former students who have gone through universities and obtained first degrees / post higher degrees.
  • And, also those students who are still at Universities studying to have their degrees (Undergraduates). Membership is not limited to Zimbabwean students but also all members of F.I.F internationally.

    2.0 NAME

    2.1.1 The name of the group shall be ZAOGA F.I.F-U.G.F

    2.1.2 The group’s operational point shall be ZAOGA F.I.F Headquarters in Waterfalls but shall be allowed to conduct its services at any other appropriate place in Zimbabwe and beyond.

    2.2 PURPOSE OF FORMATION

    2.2.1 Reaching out to the graduates with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

    2.2.2 To help the mother church in reaching lost souls, world over through members’ direct preaching and or indirect preaching by way of contributing Mission Fund and other activities needed to achieve the said purpose.

    2.2.3 To help ZOC activities through advice, spiritual guidance and any other contributions in order to meet their purpose.

    2.2.4 To come up with creative & networking platforms for the benefit of all members such as Prayer Retreats and GCCs.

    2.3 AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

    2.3.1 To win graduates to the Lord through the preaching of the gospel and other such programs.

    2.3.2 To compile an active under & postgraduates database to help in missionary & tent making for the mother church.

    2.3.3 To maintain the values and integrity of the ministry & church.

    2.3.4 To train and nurture undisputable, focused and learned leaders.

    2.3.5 To leave a substantial ministerial inheritance for the future generation.

    2.3.6 To bring to fellowship persons from different social, ethnic, economic and spiritual backgrounds.

    2.3.7 To equip Christian students with business leadership qualities.

    2.3.8 To create a sub-web page on FIFMI.org with (UGF) Graduates contact database and platform to assist the missionary office’ outreach program, for oversees contacts, tent makers and missionaries in other countries at the click of a button.

    2.3.9 Business formation and partnerships among graduates.

    .2.4 MISSION STATEMENT

    2.4.1 We are there to cultivate and nurture disciplined, faithful, God fearing and hardworking graduates premised on the motto ‘ FEAR NOT SIN NOT” who are champions of the “Go Yee Therefore” Commission and joined together by the first commandment of love.

    2.5 VALUES AND PRINCIPLES

    2.5.1 Righteousness, faithfulness and fearing of God

    2.5.2 Zero tolerance to compromising the doctrine of Christ in any way.

    2.5.3 Committed to winning of lost souls and working missions

    2.5.4 Committed to prayer.

    2.6 CODE OF CONDUCT

    (Ref. Article 3, ZAOGA FIF Guidance, Rules & Policy)

    2.6.1 Anyone who wishes to become a member of the fellowship shall be required to be born again (John3:3-8) and be filled with the Holy Spirit (Acts 2;4)

    2.6.2 Is expected to live an orderly Christian life without compromising with the bible doctrine upon which the church is centered.

    2.6.3 Every member should be faithful in giving tithes of all income to God.

    2.6.4 Every member must be faithful in attending all services

    2.6.5 Any member walking disorderly or guilty of faith in the ministry may be put under discipline by voting of the District Disciplinary Board and advisors.

    2.6.6 Every member shall be guided by the rules and policy of Z.A.O.G.A. (F.I.F.) International.
